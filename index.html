Imagine being home when you are not physically there. That is what having the ring doorbell can provide, the ability to see what happens outside of your front door or backdoor even when you are not home. This innovative doorbell has smart motion detection capabilities like no other product in the market. Back in 2012, when it was called doorbot, it was new and unassuming (Hsu, J., 2013; Siminoff, 2014). But since its beginnings, it has revolutionized the security system marketplace. In less than ten years, the Ring has gone from a team of five members to 42 members, among them 30 of the best engineers in the world. Everyone associated with the Ring works together to continue bringing the best Ring Video Doorbell product, which adds security to the home and provides it with style in all the colors to blend with the home’s exterior.

The primary mission for the Ring corporation and those who engineered the Video Doorbell has been “To Reduce Crime in Communities," (Dave, P., 2017). Yet, this product has provided so much more than that. It has offered peace of mind to millions of consumers worldwide who now own the product and know the Ring works as an extra set of eyes, providing an extra layer of protection. In addition to our mission, since its creation, there have been three product values that we continue to stand by: Convenience, Monitoring, and Security. These three values are what our customers experience and appreciate when they know the Ring is at their door.

The hope is that eventually, the Ring will protect you and everyone around you. Founder Jamie Siminoff (2014) put it best, “The name actually comes from the ‘ring’ of security we create around your home, and then in time your community.”
